ShopSuey Privacy Policy

Our mission at Shopping Made Mobile Inc. is to create a more enjoyable and rewarding shopping experience for you. To make this possible, we need to know certain information about how and where you like to shop. This document serves to explain our policies and procedures regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of the information that we collect and receive from users of our Services. Please know we have the utmost respect for your personal privacy and are committed to only use the data collected from ShopSuey users for your immediate or long-term benefit by improving the overall ShopSuey experience or providing sustainability for the Company. Unless otherwise defined herein, terms used in this Privacy Policy have the same meanings as in our Terms of Service. This Privacy Policy applies to information that is provided or collected through all the Services provided by Shopping Made Mobile Inc.

 

What Information Do We Collect?

 

Non-Personally Identifiable Information

Our Platform may automatically record information your mobile phone/device sends or transmits, including your device type, your device ID number (e.g., your UDID), your user settings, your geographical location (for example, where you are in the mall, what stores are close-by) and information about your use of the Services such as which Content you viewed, e.g., offers and events, actions you took in response to Messages you received. This information may also include which App screens you interacted with, search terms you used, date/time information and any other non-personally identifying information provided by you, e.g., your gender or date of birth. It may also include information about the use of our website, social media accounts, the Platform, and anything you authorize us to receive from other content delivery platforms (e.g., Facebook) or provided by Affiliated Partners. Collectively, this is all referred to as “Non-Personally Identifiable Information”)

 

Personal Information You Provide To Us

During your use of ShopSuey, we may ask from time to time that you provide us with certain personally identifiable information, too, e.g., your name, email, home address, or phone number, which could be used to contact or identify you (“Personal Information”). Personal Information may also include other information you supply to us concerning your preferences and interests expressed in the course of using the App or Services.

 

Information About You We May Obtain Through Other Sources

We may also receive Personal Information from Affiliated Partners you have authorized to send us data, e.g., Facebook, or from third parties (e.g., white pages listings providers), subject to the limitations of the Privacy Policies of those Affiliated Partners or third parties.

 

Information About Transactions Through Partners

To provide you with potential rewards and/or special offers, we may coordinate with third parties that may include the malls or stores you shop at, banks or credit or debit card networks or issues, or payment processors (“Partners”) to receive information about your transactions including but not limited to each transaction’s name, location, the date, time and amount of the transaction, a list or category summary of the purchased items or other personal information about you or the transaction (“Transactions”). Unless the Partner’s privacy policy you accepted already allows for this information sharing, we will not use such information in this way without your permission by your explicit opt-in.

 

When Do We Collect Information

 

While You Shop

ShopSuey is intended to function as your personal, ultimate shopping tool. To do that, we collect information while you’re shopping about where you shop, what types of offers and events you respond to, your favorite stores, brands and other behaviors and preferences. ShopSuey will track these things whenever you are physically nearby with your device and the App one of the locations in our database, e.g., a mall or merchant (“Locations”).

 

While You’re Away

Whenever you’re not close to one of our Locations, ShopSuey won’t send you Messages or track your general behavior. Our primary concern when you’re away is knowing when you’re physically nearby one of our Locations again. Thus, to know this, ShopSuey must still, at the bare minimum, collect general information about your location, comparatively to the Locations in our database. This does require your phone’s built-in GPS to be enabled and your phone turned on.

 

As Requested

Once your Account has been established, ShopSuey may occasionally request certain Personal Information during your use of the App or Services to fine-tune your experience, e.g., your name, gender or age; these questions may come in the form of a single, specific question, a survey or poll, or other formats, whether nearby or away from our Locations. The requests are always optional and at your discretion whether or not to answer.

 

How Do We Use the Information?

The terms “using” and “processing” information, as used in this policy, include using cookies or other storage means on a computer or mobile device, performing statistical analysis or collecting, storing, evaluating, modifying, deleting, using, combining, disclosing and transferring information within our organization or among our subsidiaries within the United States or internationally.

 

Non-Personally Identifiable Information

We may use Non-Personally Identifiable Information to analyze trends through aggregated demographic, psychographic or behavioral information about our users generally, and thus, for example, to improve features and functionality of the Services and to make more relevant offers to users. We may also share this Non-Personally Identifiable Information with our Partners to allow them to assist us in delivering tailored Messages, promotions, discounts, advertisements, and other benefits to you when you use the Services. Any information shared with our Partners in this context will not contain nor be linked to your Personal Information unless you have “opted in” to allow it and your information matches the criteria the Partner has specified. For example, a Partner may only want to offer a discount on its products to a certain audience (for example, men ages 25-45) or audience segment (for example, women ages 18-30 who have viewed certain content). If the Non-Personally Identifiable Information that we have collected about you meets this description, you would be able to view and use the Content offered by that Partner.

 

Personal Information

We use Personal Information only for the following purposes: (i) to personalize the behavior of your App or experience of the Services, (ii) to provide, improve and optimize the Platform, (iii) to provide you with App or Service updates or product announcements, (iv) to validate or authenticate you into your account or another service, (v) to better understand users’ needs and interests, and (vi) to occasionally provide you with further information and offers from us that we believe you may find useful or interesting, e.g., targeted advertising and promotional campaigns. We may match some of your Personal Information with your Transactions to verify or authorize certain payments or transactions. To protect your privacy, ShopSuey and our Partners exchange this information in a protected environment that does not allow our Partners to gain access to more Personal Information than is necessary for authorization.

 

Use of Cookies

We may use “cookies” (a cookie is a small data file that is kept in your device’s memory for record-keeping purposes), and/or other storage on your device to help us collect, maintain, and use this information for two purposes: (1) to make the interactions easier and more personalized for you, we may utilize persistent cookies to save information relating to your Account and/or activity for future interactions with the Services, and (2) to better understand how you interact with ShopSuey and to monitor aggregate usage by ShopSuey users and web traffic routing across our Services. Certain Partners may also place or read cookies on your browser from time to time. Although not recommended (to maintain your App’s performance), you can instruct your browser at any time, by changing its options, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit. Please understand that if you do not accept cookies, you may not be able to use all portions or all functionality of the Services.

 

Usage Patterns

We may also use data collection tags or directives such as “pixel tags,” Javascript scripts, API calls, or similar means (individually or collectively, “Usage Tracking”) in combination with cookies and the demographic, behavioral and other data we have collected, and with third-party analysis partners, to analyze usage patterns of users anonymously and to serve Messages and/or advertisements to you that are tailored based on our analysis of what we believe you would respond favorably to in light of the information that we have collected or accessed about you as described above. A “pixel tag” is an electronic image, often a single pixel and often transparent, that is placed on a web page and may be associated with cookies on your hard drive. Usage Tracking allows us to count users who have visited certain web sites and pages within web sites, or used applications or features, to analyze the behavior of users of our web sites and application, in order to improve the web sites and application, and to personalize and determine the effectiveness of promotional or advertising campaigns.
